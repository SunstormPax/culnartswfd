Waterford

Waterford is the oldest city in Ireland. Located on the river Suir, it was established by the Vikings in the 9th century as a port, and remains one of Ireland's most interesting cities to this day.

While relatively small, Wterford is exceptionally easy to travel through and contains a large ammount of attractions, making it an appealing city to live in. In comparison to larger cities such as Cork or Dublin, Waterford is much easier to find accommodation in and is relatively easy to find work in. Waterford is also directly connected to seaside towns such as Tramore and Dunmore East, allowing for easy beach trips during good weather.

Overall, Waterford is an extremely atractive city able to stand toe to toe with other, much larger cities in terms of fulfilling student's needs and is an area we cannot reccommend enough.
