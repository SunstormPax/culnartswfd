Career Opportunities

The BA (Honours) in Culinary Arts allows for a wide range of jobs and careers in all areas of the food industry.

The job most commonly attributed with this course is, of course, the chef. This course details the various skills required for cooking as well as how to manage budgets and deal with stress within the working environment. This course should allow the student any type of chef role including head chef, sous chef and specialist chef.

The course also details entrepreneurship and business management, making it ideal for those interested in opening their own business in the future.

Aside from cooking however, the course also teaches practices for areas of the food industry that don't involve cooking, including marketing, promotion, microbiology, technology and journalism. This provides access to a variety of jobs including food marketer, food critic, entrepreneur and scientist.
