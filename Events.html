Events

SETU terms generally start in September and January and end in December and April, with exam periods taking place at the end. Students will generally receive a week-long midterm break halfway through each semester, while the second semester will usually include a 2-week-long easter break approaching it's end.

During the semesters however, SETU will often feayure a number of events. One of these is freshers week, which takes place near the beginning of the first semester. Freshers week is organised by students and it's activities vary every year, but will generally include movies, competitions, bands and free food.

A second event held each year is RAG week, which is essentially semester 2's equivalent of Freshers week. It similarly features free food, competitions, music, films and is overall an extremely fun time.
