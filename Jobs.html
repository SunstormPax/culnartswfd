Jobs

Waterford is generally an easy place for students to find part-time work. Thanks to the city's university, the local business are usually willing to employ students, sometimes looking for student work specifically.

Potential job roles available to students include Sales assistants, receptionists, supervisors, assistant lecturers, cleaning operatives and waiting staff.
