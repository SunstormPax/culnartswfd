Culinary Arts Waterford

If you are in any way interested in studying Culinary Arts but heven't made up your mind as to where, then we have the course for you. The Bachelor of Arts (Honours), taught at the South Eastern Technological University (SETU), located in Waterford is one of the best options in Ireland for those wishing to enhance their culinary skills and find work in the food industry.

This site will provide details about the course, the college and the city of Waterford itself, showing you why we reccommend this course and area. This site is not affiliated with the South East Technological University. It is a fully independent detailing of why this course should not be slept on.
