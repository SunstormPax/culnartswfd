The South East Technological University (SETU)

The South East Technological University (SETU) is the only university in the south-east of Ireland. It is located on the Cork Road in Waterford City, County Waterford.

As a result of being the only university in the south east, SETU is extremely well funded and up to date on any type of facilities a student may need including an excellent library, an IT building and free broadband and wifi access throughout the entire campus. SETU also has a great number of contacts in almost every type of business in the surrounding area, making it easier for students to find work placement.

In addition to being open to international students, SETU is also part of teh Erasmus program, giving students the opportunity to study abroad during their third year as part of the course. These trips are funded by national grants.

SETU is located in Waterford, whose accommodation prices are considerably lower than those of other Irish cities, especially Dublin and Cork. The campus also provides food and catering to students for low, competitive prices.
