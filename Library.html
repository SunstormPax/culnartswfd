Library Facilities

Often considered one of the best in Ireland, the Luke Wadding Library features over 180000 printed books and 120000 e-books, covering every aspect of any course offered by the institute. Books can be taken out for a limited time for free using student cards making them extremely useful study resources both inside and outside the campus. The library also includes over 150 computers with extremely efficient wifi access which, while not offering as many programmes as those in the IT building, can act as a worthwhile alternative. The building also features various group study areas and a careers advice center for those looking for work placement.

This library is a great resource for any course including culinary arts. It features many sources detailing food regulations, journalism, accounting, entrepreneurship and international culture, making it extremely useful for several modules throughout each semester, all entirely for free.
